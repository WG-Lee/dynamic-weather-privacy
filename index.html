Dynamic Weather - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

Dynamic Weather ("the App") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

The App accesses your device location solely to display weather information for your current area. This location data is:

  • Used only to fetch weather data from the Open-Meteo API
  • Processed on your device
  • Never stored on any server
  • Never shared with third parties

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the Open-Meteo API to retrieve weather data. Your approximate location (latitude and longitude) is sent to this service to obtain weather information. Please refer to Open-Meteo's privacy policy for details on their data handling.

4. Data Storage

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All settings are stored locally on your device.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
